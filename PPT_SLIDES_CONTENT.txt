SLIDE 10: AI RANKING ALGORITHM
===========================================
Smart Ranking - Our Secret Sauce

🤖 5-Factor AI Algorithm:

1. Distance Weight (30%)
   → Closer businesses rank higher

2. Review Score (20%)
   → Better ratings = Better rank

3. Click-Through Rate (20%)
   → Popular businesses rank higher

4. User Engagement (15%)
   → More interactions = Higher rank

5. Business Activity (15%)
   → Recently updated = Fresh rank

📈 Result:
Not just "NEAREST"
But "BEST + NEAREST"

Traditional: Only distance
Our AI: Distance + Quality + Popularity + Freshness
